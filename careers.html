Why DreamSync?

DreamSync is a rapidly growing start-up with a passionate team working on cutting-edge technologies. We offer our interns the opportunity to work on real-world projects, learn from experienced mentors, and gain valuable hands-on experience.

Front-end Developer

Apply Now

DreamSync, a PIEDS-incubated BITS Goa start-up specializing in AI dubbing, voice cloning and other related services, is looking for a passionate and motivated intern to join our front-end development team.

General Information: 

  • This is a remote position.
  • Full/Part time internships are available.
  • Duration: at least 1 month. Must be able to start within 30 days
  • This is an unpaid internship.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with developing and maintaining our company website and web applications.
  • Work on new front-end development projects.
  • Test and debug web applications.
  • Learn about the latest front-end development technologies and trends.

Qualifications:

  • Strong programming skills, including HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • Experience with React is a plus.
  • Problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Passion for front-end development and a willingness to learn.

Benefits:

  • Opportunity to work on real-world front-end development projects.
  • Learn from experienced mentors.
  • Gain valuable hands-on experience.
  • Earn certificate upon completion.
  • Possibility of full-time employment.
  • Flexible hours.
